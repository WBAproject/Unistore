# UNISTORE

## Project Name
Unistore : A Platform for Buying, Selling, and Renting University Student Supplies

## Business Problem
College students have trouble buying necessary school supplies, like computers and books, because of their price and the necessity to upgrade those supplies each semester. Most school supplies have a short useable duration; therefore, making their purchase economically inefficient for many students . 

## Proposed Solution
The system is an online marketplace that enables students to buy , sell or rent academic supplies (laptops, books, and study tools ) This platform aims to provide multiple affordable options at reasonable prices, which contributes to easing the financial burden on students and enhancing the utilization of available resources among them.

## Target Audience
- University students
- Freshmen
- Students wishing to sell or rent their supplies
- Individuals looking for study supplies at reasonable prices

## System Idea
Users will have to set up an account to browse available products categorized by type (buy, sell or rent). Once a user finds an item they are looking for, they will be able to contact the seller (email and phone) and finalise the transaction on the website or by contacting the individual directly. Users will also be able to list for sale or rent items as well as find other available items.

## Key Features
- A centralized marketplace that provides a wide range of student supplies for in one place ( books, electronic devices, other materials needed to study efficiently).
- multiple options to meet students needs including Buying, Selling , and Renting .
- Easy, fast way for students to find supplies.
- Encourages reuse of used supplies instead of throwing them away.
